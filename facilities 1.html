Our Facilities

Everything your dog needs for a safe, fun and relaxing stay.

Loving Homes is designed to feel like a modern, cosy villa for dogs. Each space is carefully planned to provide comfort, safety and enrichment – just like a real holiday.

Grooming parlour and spa

A calm, hygienic space where every dog enjoys gentle bathing, brushing, nail trimming and coat care. Spa-style treatments, relaxing massages and drying keep your furry friend fresh, clean and comfortable after each stay.

Rooms with Underfloor Heating

Warm, cosy suites equipped with underfloor heating to keep your dog comfortable during rest and sleep, creating a calm and home-like environment in any weather.

Chauffeur ‘Collect & Return’ Service

A safe, convenient pick-up and drop-off option where your dog is transported comfortably by our trained chauffeur directly to and from your home.

24/7 Vet Support

Partnership with local veterinary clinics and on-call professionals for any health needs.

Secure outdoor paddocks

Spacious, fenced outdoor areas where dogs can run, play and explore safely under supervision.

Human Company & Cuddles

Our team spends one-on-one time with each guest so they feel secure, noticed and loved.
