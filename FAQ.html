Frequently Asked Questions

Hover over each question to see the answer.

What do I need to bring for my dog?
You can bring your dog’s favourite toys, blanket and any food or medication they need. We provide bowls, beds and all basic essentials during their stay.
Do you accept dogs with special needs?
Yes. We welcome dogs with medical, behavioural or dietary needs. Please inform us in advance so we can prepare the right care plan.
How often will my dog be walked?
Walks depend on the chosen package – from two walks per day in Day packages up to four walks and extra activities in Premium options.
Will I receive photo or video updates?
Yes, we send regular photos and short updates for Premium and Custom stays so you can see how relaxed and happy your dog is.
Are dogs supervised at night?
Our team monitors the suites 24/7. We check on each guest regularly and are always nearby if a dog needs attention or extra comfort.
Are the outdoor paddocks secure?
All outdoor areas are fully fenced, locked and supervised. Only compatible dogs play together, and always under staff supervision.
How can I book a stay?
You can submit a request through the Contact page, email us or call us directly. We’ll confirm dates, package type and any special requirements for your dog.
